ABOUT US

WHY WE CHOSE PHOENIX AS OUR NAME?

Phoenix is a bird from ancient Greek mythology, which has the ability to rise again from the dust after its death. From this unique ability, we take the value of being an individual who never gives up like a Phoenix who constantly rises from the dead. Phoenix is also closely related to fire which is often symbolized as a burning spirit.

WHAT IS THE MEANING OF OUR LOGO?

The shape of our logo is inspired by the Phoenix itself, which is told as a firebird who is immortal and always rises from the dead. Based on the story, we took the form of a phoenix as a symbol of unyielding. As for the Phoenix text, we put it here as an additional attribute of the logo. For the color, we use a gradient of orange and yellow which symbolizes fire. Fire itself has the meaning of a burning spirit. Our logo is also based on people's perception of the Phoenix which is symbolized as a fire bird.

WHAT IS OUR VISION & MISSION?

Our vision : Make Phoenix group remarkable and responsible towards any project which is trusted by BNCC while being activist.

Our mission :

  • Carry out tasks by instilling an attitude of responsibility and never giving up.
  • Participate in learning developments in the world of technology.
  • Strengthening relations between BNCC internal members, both inside and outside the Phoenix group.
  • Participate in advancing BNCC by actively participating in the activities held.
  • Participate in introducing BNCC to the community by helping to make the events held a success.
